Hello everyone! I am Morris Lam, and I have a keen passion for architecture and designs that appeal to the human eye.

I often find joy in exploring the intricate details of ingame architectural structures and real life buildings. For my minecraft creations, I steered myself to draw inspiration from designs from Assassin's Creed, as they are critically aclaimed for their accuracy in historical architecture. In my free time, I find satisfaction exploring the diverse range of architectural styles that are presented by the game and the real life world. .

My journey to the creation of these structures all stemmed from the creation of smaller buildings. During my younger days of playing this game, I would look up tutorials online on how people build, along with and tips and tricks on how elements they would add in order to make the world feel alive. As I grew older, I paid more attention to the architectural structures of buildings, and then decided to let my imagination loose upon Minecraft.

Aside from finding interest in designing structures that appeals to myself, I've recently found the enjoyment of designing user interfaces, which I also hope to appeal to people.
